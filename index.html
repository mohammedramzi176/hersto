Hersto Designs

Hersto is a professional mehendi designing service based in Kannur,Kerala. Inshida, founder and Chief designer at Hersto is an Engineer by profession and a mehendi artist by passion. She has been practising the art for many years. Her expertise and excellence in mehendi designing creates intricate, elegant and unique patterns on your hands, whether you are a bride to be or just celebrating special occasions.In Hersto only homemade organic Henna cones are used for clients. These are safe for skin and gives a bright maroon colour. These cones are prepared with premium quality henna powder and pure tea tree oil. Customised mehendi designs are available as per clients wish. The artist is well-versed in traditional Indian designs, Arabic designs, Khaleeji designs, Indo Arabic designs etc.
