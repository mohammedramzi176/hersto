VARIOUS DESIGNS AND STYLES

1 . Traditional Indian Mehendi : Most of the Indian brides prefer to embrace their hands and feet with traditional mehendi designs for their big day. In traditional designs intricate patterns which include peacocks, lotus designs, elephants, bride and groom faces, etc are drawn.

2 . Arabic Mehendi : Arabic mehendi designs are elegant and trendy which are preferred specially by bride's relatives and friends. There are lots of floral patterns, leaves and wines in this type of designs. Main attraction of the Arabic designs are the empty spaces within the intricate patterns.

3 . Feet Mehendi : Indian brides prefer to adorn their foot too with mehendi. Mostly symmetrical patterns are done on both feet. Customised jutis and bright Maroon mehendi detailing on foot are always a classical combination for brides.

4 . Party Mehendi : In Indian tradition, mehendi parties are important event of a wedding itself. On mehendi night, artists apply simple patterns to all the guest attending the event. The designs will be done in 5 to 10 minutes which may be a simple beil for a trendy henna tattoo.
